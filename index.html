Thesis Work


Meteorological drought pattern of Bangladesh under changing climate.


9th International Conference on Water and Flood Management-ICEMAN 2023 Dhaka, Bangladesh. ISBN- 978-984-35-4948-8
Drought needs to be explained spatially to understand its spatiotemporal variations in different areas. In my project, the meteorological drought was shown using the Standardized Precipitation Index (SPI) method and illustrated through the Inverse Distance Weighted (IDW) method across Bangladesh. We used rainfall data from 23 meteorological stations in Bangladesh during the study period of 1966–2022. For SPI calculation I used a data analysis software named R Studio. IDW and meteorological drought mapping were done in ARC GIS.
